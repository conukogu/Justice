About Us

Project Empower's Mission

Our mission is to address the challenges that domestic abuse survivors face and link them with the resources they need to enable themselves and move forward. We here at Project Empower believe that love shouldn't hurt and that nobody is safe until everybody is.

Who are we?

We are a non-profit organization located in Charlotte NC. We provide access to shelters, food, financial assistance, and health resources to help recently emancipated, recovering, or currently resisting domestic abuse survivors.
